# ADS1015 ADC
Published on 2016-06-10 in {ref}`project-11660`.

ADS1015 is a nice I2C-based analog-to-digital converter chip, with
four 12-bit channels and configurable gain. Here's a driver for it: [https://bitbucket.org/thesheep/micropython-ads1015/src](https://bitbucket.org/thesheep/micropython-ads1015/src)
