PewPew M4¶

A PewPew with a display.

The {ref}`project-159733` is pretty much where I wanted it to be in terms of balance between

size, simplicity, ease of use, functionality and, last but not least, price. However, if I sacrificed a little bit of convenience and size, maybe I could make something with more functionality?

You can get it now at  https://makerfabs.com/circuitpython-pewpew-m4.html  for $25 plus shipping.
