Pony Bot¶

A robo-pet

This is yet another attempt at a 8-servo walking robot, similar to {ref}`project-6321`  and {ref}`project-8521` . Except this time I want it to be a cute robot pet, so I had to

choose a cute shape for it. I will need to make yet another breakout board for the servos (the one I used on µKatka has some flaws) and this time I will need some sensors – not sure what yet.
