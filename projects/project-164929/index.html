PyBadge Hacking¶

Hacking on Adafruit’s PyBadge

Adafruit's PyBadge is coming soon, and I got my hands on a prototype.

Now I will do science to it.

What is PyBadge? It’s a handheld device the size of a credit card with a display, a bunch of buttons, a microcontroller running CircuitPython and a bunch of extras (neopixels, accelerometer, speaker, flash storage, battery charging, light sensor). It is also a secretly a Feather, as you can connect FeatherWings to it.

It is similar to, but much more powerful than my earlier project µGame (and the reason why I paused work on project-86818 ). I should be able to get my libraries and games from µGame to run on it, but I hope that I can also do some other fun things with it.
