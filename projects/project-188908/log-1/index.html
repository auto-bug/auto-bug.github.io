Designs Published¶

Published on 2024-10-21 in Moo Bug.

I just realized that for all this time I forgot to publish the designs files for this robot, even though I even sold some kits of it to people at the Hackaday Europe. I guess they were all too nice to ask me where they are.

In any case, both the design files and the basic walking code are now available in a repository on Github. Write me an e-mail or create an issue if you see anything missing or wrong. The JSON files are EasyEDA (standard) exports.

Enjoy!
