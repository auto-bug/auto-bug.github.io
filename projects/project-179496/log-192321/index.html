The Keyboard¶

Published on 2021-05-02 in Chocolad Keyboard Hacking.

Once the switches arrived, I was ready to assemble the keyboard. It took a little bit of detective work to figure out which way to solder the diodes (I soldered them the wrong way, but fixed it in software, thankfully SAMD21 supports both pull-ups and pull-downs on its pins), and which set of holes to use on the left and right side for the microcontroller board.
