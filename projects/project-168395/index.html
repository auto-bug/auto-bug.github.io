Keys FeatherWing¶

A FeatherWing with three keyboard keys.

Many Adafruit Feather boards are capable of simulating USB HID

devices, like keyboards and mice. So why not have a shield that makes it easy to add a small macro keyboard to your computer? Or, you know, a set or really nice buttons with color LEDs to your project.
