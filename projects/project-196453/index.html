Badge Ribbon for CircuitPython¶

Improve your paper conference badge with some blinking lights.

Some conferences offer those colorful ribbons that you can stick to

your paper badge to indicate your interests, or the fact that it’s your first time on that conference, or your pronouns, etc. I decided I want something like that to indicate that I’m interested in Python on microcontrollers, but I thought that maybe making it a little bit more flashy, with blinking lights, would be a good idea.
